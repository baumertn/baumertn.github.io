I read _Jujutsu Kaisen_ by Akutami Gege.

- Great action
- Nice art
- Some great character moments
- But not much depth.
  - That is okay, just not what I am looking for.
- The magic system seemed a bit unstructured – but maybe I didn't catch the necessary information.
  - But _Domain Expansion_ is a great concept that I have a feeling we might see more in the future – or at least referenced
- From what I've seen, I rather recommend the anime. They did a great job and even improved ond the fight scenes.

**Recommendation: Maybe**
