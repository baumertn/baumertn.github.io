I watched both _Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse_ and _Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse_ and recommend both.

Both movies have incredible aesthetics and are a joy to look at. That alone makes them worthy recommendations.

Both have perfectly adequate stories. But even for me who's not familiar with many superhero stories, they tell no new or surprising story. Which is fine.

I enjoyed the second move more than the first one.

**Recommendation: Yes**
